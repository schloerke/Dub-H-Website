What Is Dub H?

“ISU Hip Hop Dance Club, (aka Dub H), was started in Spring of 2001 by an ISU student named Carin Skowronsky. The club began with 30 members and has grown to approximately 300 members. The club was started for the purpose of allowing non-skilled, as well as advanced dancers, to have an outlet for dance expression. The intent of Dub H is to serve as an extracurricular activity and to allow each dancer to challenge themselves to their own liking.”

-- Carin Skowronsky

Dub H has danced for organizations such as the Boys & Girls Club, the ISU VEISHEA celebration and the Minnesota Timberwolves. The club has a show once at the end of each semester.

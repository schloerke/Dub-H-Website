Information Email List

This email list is for people who want to stay in touch with Dub H, but are not necessarily in the club (great for Dub H alumni and parents!). We will send out information about workshops and performances on this email list.

Subscribe

If you are not subscribed already and you want to be, send an email to majordomo@iastate.edu with the body of the email containing the text "subscribe dubh " and then your email address.

    To: majordomo@iastate.edu
    Subject: subscribe
    Body:
    subscribe dubh myemail@iastate.edu

If necessary, the email server will ask you to authorize your email account (so people can't automatically add you to their list without you knowing). The server is asking you to reply with an email with text containg your authorization code subscribe dubh and your email address.

    To: majordomo@iastate.edu
    Subject: Re: Confirmation for subscribe dubh
    Body:
    auth 1a2b3c4d subscribe dubh myemail@iastate.edu
    end

Unsubscribe

If you are on our majordomo email list and you would like to be removed, please send an email to majordomo@iastate.edu with the body of the email containing the text "unsubscribe dubh " and then your email address.

    To: majordomo@iastate.edu
    Subject: unsubscribe
    Body:
    unsubscribe dubh myemail@iastate.edu

Frequently

Asked

Questions

 
 

How much are dues?

    Dues are $15 per semester. We pay after casting.

Any other costs?

    Workshops are free. Another cost is costumes for shows, but its mostly normal clothes that you already have or could wear in the future.

Do I have to know how to dance?

    Nope. You’ll need a little bit of rhythm, some enthusiasm and a desire to learn. We can teach you the steps, but you’ve got to bring the fire.

How often do you meet?

    We have workshops during the week at the beginning of each semester. Shortly after that, we have casting. Then you have to attend specific dance’s practices. Time will vary depending on the dance.

What’s casting?

    Casting is a night where all the choreographers show part of their dances. Then you rank the dances you’d like to be in. Cabinet stays up for about 48 hours straight putting everyone into dances.

How do I join?

    Just show up to a workshop before casting to get on the email list. Show up at casting, so you can be placed in a dance. Pay your dues after you’re cast.

Learning Tips

 

                                   

2) Be loose and try to have fun with it.

3) Practice Practice Practice
    Practice at home, in your room, in the outdoors, or in a tomb. Practice with a friend, or alone, practice at practice cause you’ll remember some! That’s all Sam-I-Am.

4) Find your learning niche
    You may be a “counter” that remembers the moves in sequential order by the 8-counts. You may be “beater/music-er” and you listen to specific beats or sounds in the song. Match your learning style to the choreographer.

1) Don’t be in a crap load of dances!

    This will help you remember the smaller

number of dances you’re in and not be so

confused with other dances that may have similar moves.
